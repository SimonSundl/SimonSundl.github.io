Clubs and Leagues

Every county usually has multiple leagues filled with different clubs. For example in the league I'm playing in are 14 clubs competing against each other. Most formats provide a club having a home and an away game against each club. 3 points are awarded for a win, 1 point for each team for a draw and 0 points for a loss. Whoever is on top or bottom of the tables at the end of a season, which roughly lasts a year, will either climb to the next league or need to play one league below. The first club will win the title.
The higher the league you play the further you'll need to travel, even in other countries for the highest ones. However the lower the league the shorther the travel, because leagues are formed for smaller areas and states. The highest league includes the whole country of course, so keep that in mind.
