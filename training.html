Skills

  • Passing

    • Passing is probably one of the most important skills in football. To master the art of passing should be one of the main concerns of a football player, since football is a team game. You need to be able to pass well on any position. An attack often begins with a pass played by a defender or even the goalkeeper. In football you need to be able to decide in a very short amount of time which pass to play or prioritizing a pass over a shot on goal.

  • Shooting

    • As mentioned in passing, a good footballer knows when to shoot and when to pass. Shooting is also an art in itself and often offensive players will bring that skill to good use. Defenders usually don't have as many opportunities as an attacker, but they still can be valuable for their team if they can land a good shot. Shooting techniques are different for almost every player, but everyone has to decide if they go for a well aimed shot or putting more power in it, which depends on the situation.

  • Dribbling

    • Dribbling can also be a valuable skill. However to go for a dribble can be very risky, but rewarding if successful. To go for a dribble needs a good observation of the situation and high self esteem. When deciding to go for one, you should be sure it is successful and be wary of the consequences if gone wrong, a counterattack of the opposing team for example. Never go for dribbles if you just want to look fancy!

  • Many more

    • There are many more skills needed for football like tackling, heading or to center a ball. If you're striving to be a very good player you need to be atleast decent in all of them. The advantage of football is, if you train a lot you'll get better in all of them since you need every single skill.

Fitness

Fitness is also a very important aspect of football. Since a game lasts for 90 minutes in most cases endurance is crucial to keep the concentration up, so you'll still be useful for your team later on. I won't go into detail how to keep your fitness up, because most clubs train atleast three times a week which should be enough to keep your fitness up. If you're not convinced the training is not enough you can always go running, cycling, etc.

Tactic

Tactic plays a huge role in football. Most tactical decisions will be made by the head-coach or other coaches in the club. However a player still needs to know of formations, where to move according to his position and much more. If a beginner steps on the field he might be lost in many situations, but experience and training will help.
